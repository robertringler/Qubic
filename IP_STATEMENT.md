- **Grover's Algorithm**: Lov Grover (1996) - Published, widely implemented
- **Shor's Algorithm**: Peter Shor (1994) - Published, widely implemented
- **VQE**: Peruzzo et al. (2014) - Published research

All algorithms are implemented from public scientific literature and are not subject to patent claims by this project.
