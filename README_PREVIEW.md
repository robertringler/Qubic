## Overview

QuASIM (Quantum Approximate Solutions for Intractable Models) is a framework for evaluating quantum algorithms on classical computers. It provides tools for simulating quantum circuits and algorithms, making it a valuable resource for researchers and practitioners in quantum computing.

## Competitive Advantages

- **Robust Simulation**: QuASIM allows users to simulate complex quantum algorithms efficiently.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: The framework is designed with usability in mind, making it accessible for users of all skill levels.
- **Extensive Documentation**: Comprehensive guides and examples are available to facilitate onboarding and use.

## Core Capabilities

1. **Quantum Circuit Simulation**: Users can create and simulate quantum circuits using a variety of quantum gates and operations.
2. **Algorithm Evaluation**: QuASIM enables the evaluation of quantum algorithms in classical settings, providing insights into performance and advantages.
