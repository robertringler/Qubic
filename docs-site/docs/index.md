## What is QRATUM?

QRATUM (Quantum Resource Allocation, Tensor Analysis, and Unified Modeling) is a production-grade quantum-classical hybrid simulation framework designed for regulated industries. Formerly known as QuASIM, QRATUM provides:

- **Genuine Quantum Algorithms**: VQE for molecular simulation, QAOA for optimization
- **Aerospace Certification**: DO-178C Level A compliance
- **Defense Compliance**: NIST 800-53, CMMC 2.0 Level 2, DFARS
- **Deterministic Reproducibility**: Seed management for audit trails

!!! warning "NISQ-Era Platform"

    QRATUM is a **prototype research platform** for NISQ-era quantum computing (2025). 
    Current implementations run on classical simulators and are suitable for:
    
    - Research and algorithm development
    - Educational demonstrations  
    - Validating quantum algorithm implementations
    
    See [Quantum Capability Audit](https://github.com/robertringler/QRATUM/blob/main/QUANTUM_CAPABILITY_AUDIT.md) for detailed limitations.
