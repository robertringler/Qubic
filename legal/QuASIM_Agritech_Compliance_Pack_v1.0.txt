COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK SUMMARY
================================================================================

1. EPA FIFRA (Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act)
   - Status: Placeholder - Pesticide application compliance
   - Scope: Agricultural chemical use and environmental protection
   - Key Requirements: Application rates, buffer zones, recordkeeping

2. FDA FSMA (Food Safety Modernization Act)
   - Status: Placeholder - Food safety compliance
   - Scope: Produce safety and preventive controls
   - Key Requirements: Water quality, soil amendments, worker training

3. USDA Organic
   - Status: Placeholder - Organic certification standards
   - Scope: Organic production and handling
   - Key Requirements: Prohibited substances, buffer zones, recordkeeping

4. Good Agricultural Practices (GAP)
   - Status: Placeholder - Agricultural quality standards
   - Scope: Food safety and quality in agricultural production
   - Key Requirements: Worker hygiene, water quality, traceability
