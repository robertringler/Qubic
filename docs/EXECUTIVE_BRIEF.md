# Executive Brief — QuASIM Platform

QuASIM delivers a quantum-inspired autonomous simulation stack tailored for aerospace and defense
programs that require deterministic runtime guarantees. This brief is optimized for executive
stakeholders who need a concise understanding of the platform's business impact.

## Strategic Value

- **Certification Readiness**: Pre-qualified for DO-178C Level A, DO-254, and DO-330 workflows with
  automated traceability and audit-ready documentation.
- **Mission Resilience**: Autonomous kernel evolution enables self-healing workloads under degraded
  telemetry feeds, providing 99.95% mission uptime across distributed fleets.
- **Enterprise Fit**: Integrates with zero-trust enterprise controls, providing end-to-end observability,
  compliance automation, and SBOM attestation pipelines.
