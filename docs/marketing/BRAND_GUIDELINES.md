# QuASIM × QuNimbus Brand Guidelines

**Version 1.0** | Last Updated: November 12, 2025

## Introduction

This document defines the visual identity and brand standards for QuASIM × QuNimbus. These guidelines ensure consistent, professional, and recognizable brand presentation across all materials.

## Mission Statement

QuASIM × QuNimbus delivers enterprise-grade quantum-classical simulation with economic activation—combining aerospace certification (DO-178C Level A), validated mission data, and production-ready infrastructure to create a defensible technology moat in quantum computing.

## Brand Positioning

**What We Are**:

- The first certifiable quantum-classical platform for aerospace & defense
- Production-ready, not a research prototype
- Validated with real SpaceX and NASA mission data
- Enterprise-focused with 99.95% SLA guarantees

**What We Are Not**:

- A pure research project or academic exercise
- Focused solely on quantum hardware
- Limited to simulation without economic integration
- Unproven or experimental technology

## Visual Identity

### Logo

#### Primary Logo

The QuASIM logo consists of stylized text with quantum-inspired geometric elements.

**Versions**:

- Light version (`quasim_logo_light.svg`): For dark backgrounds (#002b36, #073642)
- Dark version (`quasim_logo_dark.svg`): For light backgrounds (#fdf6e3, white)

**Usage Rules**:

- Always maintain aspect ratio (do not stretch or distort)
- Minimum width: 120px (web), 80px (mobile), 1.5 inches (print)
- Clear space: Minimum 20px on all sides
- Do not modify colors, add effects, or rotate
- Do not place on busy backgrounds that reduce legibility

#### QuNimbus Extension

When referencing the combined platform, use:

- **Text Format**: QuASIM × QuNimbus (note the multiplication symbol ×, not letter x)
- **Alternative**: QuASIM + QuNimbus (for environments where × is unavailable)
- **Tagline**: "Quantum-Inspired Autonomous Simulation & Market-Linked Intelligence"

### Typography

#### Font Families

**Primary Font: Inter**

- **Purpose**: UI elements, marketing copy, body text, headings
- **Source**: [rsms.me/inter](https://rsms.me/inter/)
- **License**: SIL Open Font License
- **Formats**: Variable font (preferred), static weights

**Monospace Font: Roboto Mono**

- **Purpose**: Code snippets, technical specifications, data
- **Source**: [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Roboto+Mono)
- **License**: Apache 2.0
- **Formats**: Regular, Medium, Bold

#### Type Scale

| Level | Size (px) | Weight | Line Height | Usage |
|-------|-----------|--------|-------------|-------|
| H1 | 48-64 | 700 (Bold) | 1.2 | Page titles |
| H2 | 36-48 | 600 (SemiBold) | 1.3 | Section headings |
| H3 | 28-32 | 600 (SemiBold) | 1.4 | Subsection headings |
| H4 | 20-24 | 600 (SemiBold) | 1.4 | Card titles |
| Body Large | 18-20 | 400 (Regular) | 1.6 | Lead paragraphs |
| Body | 16 | 400 (Regular) | 1.6 | Standard text |
| Body Small | 14 | 400 (Regular) | 1.5 | Captions, labels |
| Code | 14 | 400 (Regular) | 1.5 | Code snippets |

#### Typography Guidelines

**Headings**:

- Use sentence case for most headings (capitalize first word only)
- Use title case for major page titles
- Maximum 2-3 lines per heading
- Maintain clear hierarchy (don't skip levels)

**Body Text**:

- Line length: 60-80 characters optimal
- Paragraph spacing: 1.5-2× line height
- Left-aligned for LTR languages
- Avoid full justification (creates uneven spacing)

**Links**:

- Color: Accent Blue (#268bd2)
- Hover: Accent Cyan (#2aa198)
- Underline: Optional (use for text-heavy contexts)
- Visited: Slightly darker Accent Blue

**Code**:

- Font: Roboto Mono
- Background: Base Medium (#073642) with 50% opacity
- Padding: 4px horizontal, 2px vertical
- Border radius: 3px
- Inline vs Block: Use inline for short snippets, block for multi-line

### Imagery

#### Photography Style

When using photography:

- **Tone**: Professional, high-tech, futuristic
- **Color Treatment**: Cool tones (blues, cyans), desaturate if needed
- **Composition**: Clean, minimalist, uncluttered
- **Subjects**: Technology, aerospace, data centers, abstract quantum concepts
- **Avoid**: Stock photos that look generic, overly warm tones, busy compositions

#### Illustrations

Custom illustrations should follow these principles:

- **Style**: Geometric, minimalist, vector-based
- **Color**: Use brand color palette
- **Complexity**: Simple shapes, clear meaning
- **Quantum Elements**: Abstract, not literal (e.g., stylized atoms, wave patterns, network graphs)

#### Icons

Use consistent icon style:

- **Library**: Feather Icons or Heroicons (both MIT licensed)
- **Style**: Outline or solid (be consistent within each context)
- **Sizes**: 24×24px (standard), 48×48px (large)
- **Color**: Match text color or use Accent Blue
- **Stroke Width**: 2px for outline icons

### Motion & Animation

#### Principles

- **Purpose**: Animations should enhance usability, not distract
- **Duration**: 200-400ms for most UI interactions
- **Easing**: Use ease-out for entrances, ease-in for exits, ease-in-out for movements
- **Subtlety**: Prefer subtle, smooth transitions over flashy effects

#### Common Animations

**Hover States**: 150ms ease-out transition on color, transform, or shadow
**Page Transitions**: 300ms fade-in
**Modal/Dialog**: 250ms scale from 0.95 to 1.0 with fade-in
**Tooltip**: 200ms fade-in with slight slide
**Loading**: Subtle pulse or rotation, infinite loop

#### Quantum-Inspired Effects

- **Glow Pulse**: Gentle pulsing of cyan glow on quantum elements (2s cycle)
- **Flow Animation**: Data particles moving along paths (3-5s loop)
- **Entanglement Lines**: Animated connections between nodes (morphing paths)

Use sparingly and ensure they don't interfere with content readability.

## Tone of Voice

### Brand Personality

- **Professional**: Enterprise-grade, credible, trustworthy
- **Innovative**: Cutting-edge, forward-thinking, pioneering
- **Technical**: Precise, accurate, scientifically rigorous
- **Accessible**: Clear explanations, avoid unnecessary jargon
- **Confident**: Assertive about capabilities, backed by validation

### Writing Style

**Do**:

- Use active voice ("QuASIM delivers..." not "Results are delivered by...")
- Be specific (metrics, numbers, concrete examples)
- Use technical terms correctly
- Explain acronyms on first use
- Focus on benefits, not just features

**Don't**:

- Overhype or make unsupported claims
- Use buzzwords without substance
- Over-simplify complex concepts (maintain technical accuracy)
- Use exclamation points excessively
- Compare negatively to competitors (focus on our strengths)

### Key Messages

1. **Certification Moat**: Only quantum platform with DO-178C Level A certification posture
2. **Mission Validated**: Proven with SpaceX Falcon 9 and NASA Orion/SLS telemetry (<2% RMSE)
3. **Production Ready**: 99.95% SLA, multi-cloud Kubernetes, enterprise infrastructure
4. **Economic Integration**: QuNimbus links quantum efficiency to enterprise valuation
5. **Autonomous Evolution**: Phase III RL optimization with formal verification

### Messaging Framework

**For Aerospace/Defense Customers**:

- Emphasis: Certification, compliance, mission validation
- Proof points: DO-178C Level A, CMMC 2.0 L2, SpaceX/NASA results
- Tone: Highly technical, rigorous, formal

**For Enterprise Customers**:

- Emphasis: Production readiness, ROI, integration ease
- Proof points: 99.95% SLA, Fortune 500 pipeline, GPU acceleration
- Tone: Professional, business-focused, benefit-driven

**For Technical Audiences**:

- Emphasis: Architecture, performance, innovation
- Proof points: cuQuantum integration, tensor networks, RL optimization
- Tone: Technical, detailed, peer-to-peer

**For Investors**:

- Emphasis: Market opportunity, competitive moat, traction

## Compliance & Legal

### Trademark Usage

- QuASIM™ and QuNimbus™ are trademarks (use ™ symbol on first prominent use)
- Do not modify or abbreviate trademark names
- Use proper capitalization: QuASIM, not QUASIM or quasim

### Third-Party Logos

When featuring partner or customer logos:

- Obtain written permission
- Follow partner's brand guidelines
- Maintain clear space around logos
- Do not imply endorsement without agreement

### Export Control

QuASIM development must comply with ITAR and EAR:

- **Do not** include export-controlled information in public materials
- **Do not** specify capabilities that exceed public domain
- **Do verify** with legal team before publishing technical specifications

### Accessibility

All materials must meet WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards:

- Color contrast: Minimum 4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text
- Text alternatives: Provide alt text for images
- Keyboard navigation: Ensure all interactive elements are accessible
- Readable fonts: Minimum 16px for body text
